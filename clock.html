AM vs PM

A day has 24 hours, but a clock only shows 12 numbers. So the clock goes around twice each day!

AM (ante meridiem) = Midnight (12:00) to just before Noon (11:59)

PM (post meridiem) = Noon (12:00) to just before Midnight (11:59)
